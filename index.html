Password Tips

1. Length Matters

Use passwords that are at least 12 characters long. Longer passwords are generally more secure.

2. Mix Characters

Include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols for a stronger password.

3. Avoid Common Words

Avoid using easily guessable words or phrases . Opt for a combination of random names and characters.
